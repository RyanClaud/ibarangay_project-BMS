## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Each Role

### For Admins:
- Create accounts for all officials
- Train each person on their role
- Monitor system regularly
- Help troubleshoot issues

### For Captains:
- Review requests daily
- Be fair and consistent
- Check resident information before approving
- Reject with clear reasons

### For Secretaries:
- Process requests quickly
- Keep resident records updated
- Communicate with residents
- Release documents promptly after payment

### For Treasurers:
- Verify payments carefully
- Issue receipts immediately
- Keep accurate records
- Generate financial reports regularly

### For Residents:
- Submit complete requirements
- Pay fees promptly
- Check request status online
- Keep profile information current

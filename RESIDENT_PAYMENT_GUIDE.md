## ğŸ“± Step-by-Step: How to Upload Payment

### Step 1: Request Gets Approved
- Captain approves your request
- Status changes to **"Approved"**
- You see a blue **"Upload Payment"** button

### Step 2: Make Payment
**Choose your payment method**:

#### Option A: GCash
1. Open GCash app
2. Send Money â†’ Enter barangay GCash number
3. Amount: â‚±50.00 (or amount shown)
4. Add reference: Your tracking number
5. Confirm payment
6. **Take screenshot** of success message

#### Option B: PayMaya
1. Open PayMaya app
2. Send Money â†’ Enter barangay PayMaya number
3. Amount: â‚±50.00 (or amount shown)
4. Confirm payment
5. **Take screenshot** of receipt

#### Option C: Bank Transfer
1. Use online banking or visit bank
2. Transfer to barangay account
3. Amount: â‚±50.00 (or amount shown)
4. Get receipt
5. **Take photo** of receipt

### Step 3: Upload Payment Proof
1. Go to your document requests
2. Find the approved request
3. Click **"Upload Payment"** button (blue button in status column)
4. Fill in the form:
   - **Payment Method**: Select (GCash, PayMaya, etc.)
   - **Reference Number**: Enter transaction ID
   - **Account Name**: Your name (optional)
   - **Payment Date**: When you paid
   - **Upload Proof**: Click and select your screenshot
5. Preview your screenshot
6. Click **"Submit Payment"**

### Step 4: Wait for Verification
- Status changes to **"Payment Submitted"**
- Treasurer will verify your payment
- Usually takes 24 hours

### Step 5: Get Your Receipt
- After verification, status becomes **"Payment Verified"**
- Click **"View Receipt"** button
- Print or download your official receipt
- Keep receipt for claiming document

### Step 6: Pick Up Document
- Status changes to **"Ready for Pickup"**
- Visit barangay office
- Bring: Valid ID + Receipt
- Get your document
- Status changes to **"Released"**

## ğŸ” Troubleshooting

### "I don't see Upload Payment button"
**Check**:
- Is your status "Approved"? (not Pending)
- Are you logged in as the resident who made the request?
- Try refreshing the page

### "Upload button is grayed out"
**Reason**: You may have already uploaded payment
**Check**: Status should be "Payment Submitted"

### "File upload failed"
**Possible reasons**:
- File too large (max 5MB)
- Not an image file (must be JPG/PNG)
- Internet connection issue
**Solution**: Try smaller file or different format

### "Payment was rejected"
**What to do**:
1. Check rejection reason
2. Verify your payment details
3. Upload correct proof
4. Status goes back to "Approved"
5. Upload button appears again
